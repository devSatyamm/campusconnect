## The Problem Nobody Talks About

> *"There was a hackathon last weekend. I heard about it on Monday."*

Sound familiar?

Inside every university, things are constantly happening  hackathons, study groups, football matches, music jams, startup idea sessions. But the information to find them is buried in:

| Channel | Problem |
|---|---|
| ūüďĪ WhatsApp groups | You have to be in the right one |
| ūüďł Instagram stories | Gone in 24 hours |
| ūüďĆ Posters | You have to walk past them |
| ūüďß Emails | Lost in the inbox |
| ūüó£ÔłŹ Word of mouth | Luck-based |

**The result?** The most engaged 10% of students know everything. The rest miss most of it.

There is no single place where a student can open an app and see:
- What is happening on campus **today**?
- What can I **join**?
- Who shares my **interests**?

## What CampusConnect Does

**CampusConnect** is a centralized student event platform ‚ÄĒ built to turn fragmented campus information into a structured discovery experience.

```
Student creates event  ‚Üí  Platform surfaces it  ‚Üí  Interested students find & join it
```

Starting with **Manipal University Jaipur (MUJ)** as the pilot campus.

## Core Product Principles

### ūüĒź 1. Trust-First Onboarding
Login restricted to **official university email IDs** only.
- Ensures a real student community
- Reduces spam from day one
- Builds credibility without moderation overhead

### ūüß≠ 2. Clean Discovery Interface
Minimal event feed. Clear cards. Quick join actions. Every design decision optimized for **low-friction interaction** ‚ÄĒ because friction kills discovery.

### ūüôĆ 3. Student-Driven Ecosystem
No gatekeeping. Any student can create an event. Clubs, individuals, random people with an idea ‚ÄĒ all equal. Encourages initiative over passive consumption.

### ūüĎ• 4. Social Layer Built In
**Best Friends** ‚ÄĒ a feature that automatically surfaces the people you interact with most, based on shared events and activity. Real connections over follower counts.

### ūüĆź 5. Web-First Architecture
No app store. No install. Works on mobile, laptop, and tablet. **Zero installation barrier.**

### ūüďą 6. Built to Scale
Starting at MUJ. Architecture designed so any university can have its own verified community environment ‚ÄĒ same product, isolated communities.
